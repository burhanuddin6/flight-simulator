Curved Flight Simulator

The curvature is intentionally introduced because we thought it would be more fun (realistic?) to fly.

The simulator is slow because we are generating new randomized terrain every frame.

Controls: Please note that if a control is not working, you may need to click on the canvas first.

Esc: Quit the program

Space: Increase speed

1: Move left

2: Move right

3: Move up

4: Move down

5: Decrease camera far plane

6: Increase camera far plane

W: Pitch up

S: Pitch down

A: Yaw left

D: Yaw right

Q: Roll left

E: Roll right

C: Toggle shading mode

V: Toggle view mode

Any other key: Reset speed factor
